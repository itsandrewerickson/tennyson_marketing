# Product Vision: Tennyson Street Offer Memorandum

## Executive Summary

### The Problem
Selling a property with complex development potential requires more than a standard listing. Developers need to understand the opportunity, the numbers, and the regulatory advantage before they'll engage.

### The Solution
A professional Offer Memorandum package that:
1. Tells the story of a rare grandfathered development opportunity
2. Provides clear financial projections for different exit strategies
3. Captures qualified leads through a modern landing page
4. Delivers architectural plans instantly to serious buyers

### Core Goals
1. **Generate qualified leads** from developers and ambitious owner-occupiers
2. **Communicate scarcity** - these ADU rights cannot be replicated post-Summer 2025
3. **Reduce tire-kickers** by being upfront about price and opportunity
4. **Accelerate deal velocity** by providing all materials digitally

---

## Product Philosophy

### "Professional but Approachable"
- Match the quality of institutional OMs (Colliers, CBRE, Marcus & Millichap)
- But speak to smaller developers and owner-occupiers, not just funds
- Clear, jargon-light language
- Strong visuals over dense text

### "Show the Upside"
- Lead with the development potential, not just current state
- Multiple profit scenarios (build & hold, build & sell, etc.)
- Make the grandfathered advantage unmistakably clear

---

## Deliverables

### 1. Offer Memorandum PDF
**Sections:**
1. Cover / Hero Image
2. Executive Summary (The Opportunity)
3. Property Overview (Existing House + ADU Teardown)
4. Development Potential (8 ADUs, unit mix, parking)
5. The Grandfathered Advantage (regulatory context)
6. Location & Neighborhood (Point Loma lifestyle)
7. Financial Analysis
   - Pro Forma (current vs. stabilized)
   - Profit Forecast (3 scenarios)
   - Comparable Sales
8. Site Plans & Renderings
9. Contact / Next Steps

**Design Notes:**
- Landscape orientation (spreads format like Colliers example)
- Dark header bars with gold/cream accents
- High-quality photography prominent
- Clean data tables

### 2. Landing Page
**Sections:**
1. Hero with property image + headline
2. Key highlights (price, units, location)
3. Photo gallery
4. The Opportunity (grandfathered story)
5. Email capture form → auto-delivers plans PDF
6. Contact information

**Technical:**
- React/Next.js
- Tailwind CSS
- Deployed on Vercel
- Simple email capture (no complex CRM needed)
