## The Grandfathered Advantage

### Regulatory Context
- **Effective Date**: Summer 2025
- **Change**: ADUs can no longer be built on cul-de-sac properties in San Diego
- **Impact**: These 8 approved ADUs are grandfathered under previous rules
- **Scarcity**: This development potential cannot be replicated on similar properties

### Key Selling Points
1. Plans locked in before regulatory change
2. 8-unit development on a cul-de-sac - now impossible to permit
3. Mix of market rate (5) and deed restricted (3) satisfies city requirements
4. Vaulted ceilings add premium feel to compact units
